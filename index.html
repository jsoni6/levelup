Customer Service

Why and How: Have you ever sat through an entire meeting and once it ended you had no idea what just happened or what the main focus of the whole meeting was about? Well if you have then that is a prime example of the wrong way of effective communication. When communicating the whole point of the conversation is to convey an idea to a person or group. Without this skill any type of pitch you give to anyone is practically useless. So over the course of this lesson we will learn to communicate effectively in a short and simple manner. During this lesson there will be a lot of class participation and demonstrations and the class will be able to come up with their own scenarios and show them to the class. Each demonstration of the scenarios will be assigned a problem and each group must demonstrate an incorrect way to handle the problem and then demonstrate the correct way they handled the problem. After each demonstration each group must explain the changes they made to correctly handled/solved the problem. Students may also provide feedback and ask questions to the way the situations are handled and how they went about making their decisions.
